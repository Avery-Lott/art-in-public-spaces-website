Our mission is to bring together students, staff, and faculty across disciplines and work with professional artists, designers, and engineers to bring cutting-edge art to public spaces on the CU Boulder campus.

By creating art in public spaces throughout the CU Boulder Campus, we will engage students, faculty, staff, and campus visitors with an enriching and educational experience that will create dialogue, community, and understanding about the power of art and the creative process.
